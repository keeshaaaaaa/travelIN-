Itinerary



  • Kochi


    Arrive in Kochi, the vibrant gateway to Kerala. Check into a charming homestay in Fort Kochi, known for its colonial history and eclectic atmosphere. Enjoy a leisurely stroll along the historic streets, visit the iconic Chinese Fishing Nets, and savor traditional Kerala cuisine at a local restaurant.

  • Munnar (2 nights)


    Depart for Munnar, a hill station renowned for its tea plantations and scenic beauty. Visit the Tea Museum to learn about tea processing and enjoy a stroll through the lush tea gardens. Explore the Mattupetty Dam and Kundala Lake for boating and picturesque views.

    Spend the second day exploring Munnar's natural beauty. Visit Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr, and trek to Rajamalai viewpoint for stunning panoramas. Explore the Echo Point and Top Station for breathtaking views of the Western Ghats. Overnight stay for both nights in Munnar.


  • Alleppey (Alappuzha)


    Drive to Alleppey, also known as the Venice of the East, famous for its backwaters and houseboat cruises. Check into a houseboat and enjoy a serene cruise through the backwaters, witnessing rural life along the banks. Overnight stay in the houseboat.


  • Alleppey to Kumarakom


    Disembark from the houseboat and head to Kumarakom, another backwater destination. Visit the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, a haven for birdwatchers with various migratory birds. Enjoy activities like boating, fishing, and Ayurvedic massages at your resort. Overnight stay in Kumarakom.


  • Thekkady


    Depart for Thekkady, home to the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Take a boat safari on Periyar Lake to spot wildlife like elephants, tigers, and birds. Visit spice plantations and learn about the cultivation of various spices like cardamom, pepper, and cloves. Enjoy a cultural show in the evening showcasing traditional art forms. Overnight stay in Thekkady.


  • Departure


    Drive back to Kochi for departure. Our job is to show you a glimpse of Kerala's diverse landscapes, from lush hill stations to tranquil backwaters, ensuring a memorable and enriching travel experience.
